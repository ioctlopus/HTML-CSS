Information about pterodactyls

Virtually all pterodactyls are gone now. The one living
example of a pterodactyl can be found at the one and only
Jurassic Park© theme park.

Information about Markov chains

Markov chains are stochastic models used to demonstrate  cause-and-effect probabilistic
relationships between elements of a system. Markov chains are
widely used in many fields of science and mathematics.
